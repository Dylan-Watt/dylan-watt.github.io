In this page i am going to explain the difference between Margin, Border and padding.

Margin border and padding are all fundamentals in coding they define how elements are shown on a webpage.

We start with content this box is in the very center of boxes this is where all your base content goes

Next we have Border this is a line that surrounds the pading and the content. this is the very outside of the webpage the final border. Margin is the transparent space on the outside of the border. margins are used to maintain the distance between the elements preventing them from overlapping or touching.

And last but not least we have padding this creates a space between the content and the element this creates inner padding to create room around the element its self
